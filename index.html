About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Rhett, I'm a full-stack developer & designer with 8+ years of professional experience.

I really enjoy working on projects that live on the Internet, from designing & developing entire websites, to creating highly interactive & visual applications. I pride myself on my creativity and pixel-perfect attention to detail.

In May 2020, I graduated from Boise State University with a B.S. Computer Science degree. During my studies, I maintained full-time employment, working in a variety of different roles, including as a website designer & developer, support analyst, documentation specialist, and most-recently as a front-end engineer.
